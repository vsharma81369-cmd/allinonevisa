Radical honesty

What's not included.

Because the surprises should all be good ones. These costs are set by the destination country and paid directly to them, never to us.

Government fees

Every embassy charges its own visa fee. We show you the exact amount up front and you pay it directly to them.

Biometrics appointments

Fingerprints and photos taken at an official center carry a separate fee, scheduled at your convenience.

In‑person requirements

Some visas require an interview or a visit you must attend yourself. We prepare you for it, but we can't go in your place.
